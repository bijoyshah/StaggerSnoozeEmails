Gmail Stagger Snooze — Installation Instructions
==================================================

WHAT YOU NEED
-------------
- Google Chrome browser
- This folder (you already have it!)


STEPS
-----
1. Open Google Chrome

2. Type this in the address bar and hit Enter:
      chrome://extensions/

3. In the top right corner, turn on "Developer mode"
   (it's a toggle switch — click it so it turns blue)

4. Click the "Load unpacked" button
   (it appears on the left after you enable Developer mode)

5. A file picker will open — find and select this folder
   (the one containing this file) and click Open

6. You should see "Gmail Stagger Snooze" appear on the page
   with a blue toggle — make sure it's turned on

7. Go to Gmail (mail.google.com)

8. Look for the 💤 button in the bottom right corner of Gmail
   Click it to get started!


THAT'S IT!
----------
You do not need to create any accounts or install anything else.
The extension runs entirely in your browser.

If you have questions about how to use it, click the ❓ button
inside the extension panel once it's installed.


TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
- Don't see the 💤 button? Try refreshing Gmail after installing.
- Extension not loading? Make sure Developer mode is turned ON
  at chrome://extensions/ and the toggle next to the extension is blue.
- Something broken after a Gmail update? Go to chrome://extensions/,
  find Gmail Stagger Snooze, and click the refresh icon on its card.
  Then reload Gmail.
